About HDA

The Hyderabad Development Authority is the primary planning and development body responsible for sustainable urban development, land management, and infrastructure growth in Hyderabad.

Who We Are

Hyderabad Development Authority: Building Tomorrow's City

The Hyderabad Development Authority (HDA) is a statutory authority established to plan, regulate, and facilitate the development of Hyderabad as a modern, organized, and sustainable urban center. HDA operates under the Hyderabad Development Authority Ordinance 1976 and works in coordination with provincial and federal government agencies.

Our mandate encompasses land acquisition, infrastructure development, public utility management, and regulatory oversight. Through strategic planning, transparent governance, and stakeholder engagement, HDA aims to create a balanced urban environment that serves the diverse needs of residents, businesses, and communities while preserving Hyderabad's cultural heritage.

City Planning

Hyderabad’s urban planning focuses on balanced residential, commercial, and recreational spaces, with an emphasis on mobility, safety, and access to essential services.

Heritage & Culture

The city preserves its historic landmarks and cultural identity while supporting new development that reflects Hyderabad’s spirit.

Infrastructure

Investment in roads, utilities, public transport, and community amenities helps Hyderabad grow responsibly and serve its people better.

Future Growth

Hyderabad aims to expand sustainably with smart land management, environmental stewardship, and strong governance for long-term prosperity.
